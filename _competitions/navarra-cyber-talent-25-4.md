The Navarra Cyber Talent CTF is an official competition showcased within the **I Navarra Cybersecurity Week**, an initiative organized by the Navarra Cybersecurity Center and celebrated from October 20 to 24 in conjunction with European Cybersecurity Month.

The event aims to promote the culture of cybersecurity across Navarra, raising digital maturity and encouraging societal engagement by bringing together institutions, citizens, businesses, educators, and public administrations.
