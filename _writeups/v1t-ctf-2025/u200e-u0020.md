## Exploitation

When you encounter a file that uses only two distinct types of characters, it's reasonable to suspect they represent binary digitsâ€”`0` and `1`. Typically, the first step is to determine which character corresponds to which bit. Since there are only two possibilities, you can quickly try both combinations to identify the correct mapping.
